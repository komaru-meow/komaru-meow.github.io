Unlocking the bootloader

First of all, you will need to unlock the bootloader to allow modifications to the OS. To begin, make sure you have the SIM card inserted. After you're done, go to Settings > About phone and tap on the OS version until developer options get enabled. If you have a password, you'll be prompted to enter it. After you do that, go to Settings > Additional settings > Developer options. In there, enable OEM unlocking and USB debugging. Go to "Mi unlock status", switch to your mobile date (You can't use Wi-FI for this) and click on "Add account and device".
